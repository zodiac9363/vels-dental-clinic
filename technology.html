State of the Art

Advanced Dental Technology

At Vels Dental Clinic, we invest in the latest dental technology to ensure our patients receive the most precise, comfortable, and efficient care possible. From 3D imaging to laser treatments, experience the future of dentistry today.

Laser Dentistry

Our advanced dental lasers replace traditional drills for many procedures, offering a pain-free, bloodless experience with significantly faster healing times.

Basal Implants

Utilizing cutting-edge Swiss technology, we offer Immediate Loading Basal Implants. Get your new teeth fixed in just 72 hours, even with minimal bone density.

Invisalign Clear Aligners

We use 3D digital scanning to create custom Invisalign clear aligners, offering a virtually invisible and highly comfortable way to straighten your teeth.

Class-B Autoclave Sterilization

Patient safety is our highest priority. We use hospital-grade Class-B Autoclave sterilization protocols to ensure all instruments are 100% bacteria-free.

Digital RVG X-Rays

Our RadioVisioGraphy (RVG) digital x-ray systems emit 90% less radiation than traditional x-rays while providing instant, high-definition images for accurate diagnosis.

Rotary Endodontics

We perform root canal treatments using advanced rotary endodontic handpieces and apex locators, making the procedure faster, highly precise, and completely painless.
