About Vels Dental Clinic & Dr. Balaji Velayutham

Delivering premium dental care to Thanjavur with advanced technology and a gentle touch.

Meet Dr. Balaji Velayutham

Dr. Balaji Velayutham is the Chief Dentist at Vels Dental Clinic, Thanjavur. He completed his Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS) from the prestigious Saveetha Dental College, Chennai, graduating with First Class distinction.

With over 17 years of clinical experience, Dr. Balaji has been providing comprehensive dental care with a strong commitment to patient comfort and quality treatment. He has further enhanced his expertise by obtaining an Advanced Degree in Implantology from Rajan Dental Institute, Chennai, and is also a Certified Laser Dentist trained at Saveetha Dental College.

At Vels Dental Clinic, our mission is to serve people from all walks of life by providing high-quality dental care at reasonable and affordable charges. We firmly believe that everyone deserves access to modern dental treatments without financial burden.
