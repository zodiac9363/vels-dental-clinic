Medical Team

Meet Our Specialist Dental Team in Thanjavur

Our multidisciplinary team of 6 specialist dentists provides comprehensive, specialized care tailored to your unique needs.

Chief Dentist / Implantologist

Dr. Balaji Velayutham

BDS · Certified Implantologist · Certified Laser Dentist

Dr. Balaji Velayutham is the Chief Dentist at Vels Dental Clinic, Thanjavur. He completed his Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS) from the prestigious Saveetha Dental College, Chennai, graduating with First Class distinction.

With over 17 years of clinical experience, Dr. Balaji has been providing comprehensive dental care with a strong commitment to patient comfort and quality treatment. He has further enhanced his expertise by obtaining an Advanced Degree in Implantology from Rajan Dental Institute, Chennai, and is also a Certified Laser Dentist trained at Saveetha Dental College.

At Vels Dental Clinic, our mission is to serve people from all walks of life by providing high-quality dental care at reasonable and affordable charges. We firmly believe that everyone deserves access to modern dental treatments without financial burden.

Book with Dr. Balaji
Oral & Maxillofacial Surgeon

Dr. E. Eswar

MDS (Oral Surgery)

Dr. Eswar is a highly qualified Oral and Maxillofacial Surgeon with extensive expertise in the surgical extraction of teeth, including the management of impacted teeth and other complex oral surgical procedures. He serves as the Implantologist at our clinic and has vast experience in both Basal Implants and Conventional Dental Implants.

Our clinic offers advanced implant solutions, including immediate implant placement and immediate crown delivery whenever clinically appropriate, helping patients restore their smile and function with minimal treatment time.

Our commitment is to provide safe, effective, and affordable dental care using the latest techniques and technology.

Book with Dr. Eswar
Periodontist

Dr. M. B. Narayanan

MDS (Periodontics)

Dr. Narayanan is our clinic's consultant periodontist, specializing in the diagnosis and treatment of gum and bone-related diseases of the oral cavity. He excels in the management of bleeding gums, bad breath (oral malodor), and loose or mobile teeth, providing patients with effective and long-lasting solutions.

An experienced flap surgeon, Dr. Narayanan is highly skilled in advanced periodontal procedures aimed at restoring and maintaining optimal gum health. He also offers laser-assisted flap surgery, ensuring precise, comfortable, and affordable treatment for our patients. His dedication to quality care helps patients achieve healthier gums and a stronger foundation for their teeth.

Book with Dr. Narayanan
Orthodontist

Dr. Yasodharan

MDS (Orthodontics)

Dr. Yasodharan is our consultant Orthodontist, dedicated to creating beautiful, confident smiles at affordable costs. He is a Certified Invisalign Provider and has special expertise in Damon Braces, an advanced American orthodontic technology known for providing comfortable and efficient treatment.

We offer a wide range of orthodontic solutions, including:

  • Invisalign Clear Aligners
  • Damon Braces
  • Metal Braces
  • Ceramic (Tooth-Coloured) Braces
  • Myofunctional Appliances

Dr. Yasodharan specializes in correcting crowded, crooked, misaligned, and irregularly spaced teeth, helping patients achieve a healthy bite and a confident, attractive smile. Our goal is to provide high-quality orthodontic care that is effective, comfortable, and affordable for all.

Book with Dr. Yasodharan
Endodontist / General Dentist

Dr. Kamali Kasinathan

BDS · Specialist in Root Canal

Dr. Kamali Kasinathan is a junior dentist at our clinic. She is a skilled general dentist who excels in providing preventive and restorative dental care, including scaling and polishing, tooth extractions, tooth-colored fillings, and root canal treatments. She is dedicated to delivering high-quality dental care with utmost kindness, compassion, and attention to patient comfort. Her gentle approach and commitment to patient well-being help ensure a positive and stress-free dental experience for all.

Book with Dr. Kamali
Pedodontist

Dr. Dimple J

MDS (Pediatric Dentistry)

Dr. Dimple J is our clinic's consultant Pedodontist and Preventive Dentist, specializing in comprehensive dental care for children. She has exceptional skills in managing and treating children with patience, kindness, and compassion. Even when children are anxious or uncooperative, she helps them feel comfortable through her gentle and child-friendly approach.

Her areas of expertise include:

  • Child behavior management
  • Pediatric root canal treatments
  • Tooth extractions for children
  • Pediatric crowns
  • Fluoride applications for the prevention of dental caries (tooth decay)
  • Habit-breaking appliances for conditions such as thumb sucking and mouth breathing

Dr. Dimple is dedicated to providing a positive dental experience for every child, ensuring their oral health is maintained with the utmost care and attention.
