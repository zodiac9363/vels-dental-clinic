FAQ

Get clear answers to your questions

How often should I visit the dentist?
We recommend a check-up and professional cleaning every 6 months. However, your dentist may suggest more frequent visits depending on your oral health needs.
Learn more
Are dental treatments painful?
Modern dentistry has made treatments largely pain-free. With proper local anaesthesia and our gentle approach, most procedures at Vels Dental Clinic are comfortable and stress-free.
Do you offer dental implants?
Yes. Dr. E. Eswar, our Oral & Maxillofacial Surgeon, performs both Basal Implants and Conventional Implants with immediate crown delivery wherever clinically possible.
Do you treat children?
Absolutely. Dr. Dimple J is our specialist Pedodontist, trained specifically in children's behaviour management and paediatric dental care for all ages.
What are your clinic hours?
We are open Monday to Saturday: 10:00 AM – 1:00 PM and 6:00 PM – 9:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays.
